FAQs

Can you help us with our event and activation designs?

We can do 2D site/floor plans, 3D presentation renders for your pitch decks, animations and flyovers to present shape/scale, and working drawings. We also do a nice napkin sketch - which is sometimes all the project needs to get things moving!

Do you hire equipment?

We hire a variety of event equipment, from site signage, weights, truss, entry arches and much more.

We already have a concept, can you produce it?

A lot of our work comes from existing creative - so the answer to this is certainly a yes. If you have a concept or creative already, then we can start with that and see where we might be able to assist in producing a finished product.

We don't do events, but we want to - can you help?

We certainly can - whether its trade show, expo’s, music festivals, existing partnerships/venues, retail sites or more, we can make help design and produce something that suits the environment and market that it’s going in to.

Do you work directly with brands, or other agencies, and do you do above the line marketing?

We enjoy working directly with young, enthusiastic brands who are beginning their event and activation journeys - however, we don’t tend to do campaign creative or any above the line marketing, which is typically in the domain of a brands internal team or marketing agency. Experiential and creative event production (our area of expertise) is what we like to do, and is what we’re good at. So often our projects land on our desk via PR or other marketing agencies, but occasionally directly via the brands themselves. If you think we might be the right fit for your project - give us a shout, if the brief doesn’t suit us - we’ll probably be able to point you in the direction of some other trusted and established industry experts.

We aren’t getting value out of our activations at the moment, can you help?

Value is what we’re about - and if you’re not getting it by activating and we can help it - we will. More often than not activating a brand at an event is an expensive exercise, but there’s usually some ways to audit the costs and bring them down. Send us an email and we’ll need how we can help.

Do you work all over Australia?

Whilst we’re based in Melbourne, we do events and activations all over Australia
